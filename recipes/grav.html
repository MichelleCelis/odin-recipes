GRAVLAX

If Odin had a go-to snack while pondering the mysteries of the runes, this would be it. Gravlax: the dish that swam through Nordic seas just to make it to your feast! Get ready to plunder its rich, salty, and herb-infused depths. Sk√•l!

INGREDIENTS

  • 450g fresh salmon fillet, skin on
  • 120g salt
  • 120g sugar
  • 1 bunch fresh dill, chopped
  • 5g crushed black pepper
  • 15g lemon zest

METHOD

  1. In a bowl, mix together the salt, sugar, black pepper, and lemon zest.
  2. Take half of this mixture and rub it generously onto the flesh side of the salmon.
  3. Lay the chopped dill over the salmon.
  4. Sprinkle the remaining salt mixture over the dill, making sure it's well-covered.
  5. Wrap the salmon tightly in cling film.
  6. Place the wrapped salmon in a dish and send it off to fridge for 48-72 hours. Turn the salmon every 12 hours.
  7. Remove the salmon from the fridge, discard the dill, and thinly slice to serve.
