FISH STEW

When you've pillaged all day and yearn for a hearth to call your own, there's no better comfort than a bubbling pot of fish stew. This fish and root vegetable stew is a celestial blend of flavours that even Odin would approve of.

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g firm white fish (such as cod or haddock), cubed
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 750ml fish or vegetable broth
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/2tsp black pepper
  • 2tsps paprika
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

METHOD

  1. Heat a bit of oil in a large pot and saut√© the onion and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add the fish to the pot and season with salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir gently to coat the fish.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to a simmer, cover, and let it cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Add the cubed potatoes and sliced carrots, continuing to simmer for an additional 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Taste for seasoning and serve piping hot.
