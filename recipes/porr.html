BARLEY PORRIDGE

Ah, seekers of ancient wisdom, gather 'round for Odin's Wisdom Bowl - a barley porridge as rich in flavor as the Allfather is in knowledge! Simmered in milk and kissed by honey and cinnamon, this nourishing bowl is fit for gods and mortals alike. May it fuel your quests and fill your hearts!

INGREDIENTS

  • 240g pearl barley
  • 950ml milk
  • 60ml honey
  • 5ml cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: dried fruit or nuts for topping

METHOD

  1. Thoroughly rinse the pearl barley under cold water.
  2. In a large pot, bring the milk to a gentle boil.
  3. Add the cleansed barley and a pinch of salt to the boiling milk.
  4. Lower the heat to a simmer, stirring every so often for 45-60 minutes, until the barley becomes soft and has soaked up most of the milk.
  5. Add the honey and cinnamon, blend well.
  6. Place the wrapped salmon in a dish and send it off to fridge for 48-72 hours. Turn the salmon every 12 hours.
  7. Serve your Wisdom Bowl warm, topped with optional dried fruits or nuts if you desire.
