BERRY MEAD

What do skalds sip while composing epic tales? Why, berry mead, of course! A nectar so divine it could make even the most grizzled Viking warrior recite poetry. Drink up, but beware, the verses you start spouting might just be immortalized in the Eddas!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1L water
  • 340ml honey
  • 60g mixed berries
  • 1/4 packet mead yeast
  • 1/4tsp yeast nutrient

METHOD

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add honey and stir until fully dissolved.
  3. Add mixed berries and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Let the mixture cool to room temperature.
  5. Add the yeast and yeast nutrient.
  6. Transfer to a sterilised fermenter and seal with an airlock.
  7. Let ferment for 4-6 weeks.
  8. Once fermentation is complete, bottle and age for at least a month before enjoying.
